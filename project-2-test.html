Where have all the wild pokémon gone!?

Detective pikachu wants to help you search and complete your pokédex!

Where to start, with over 800 pokémon over 8 generations, that's a lot of pokemon to catch!

Let's use Detective Pikachu's locator tool to find the stats and locations of the pokemon on your list!

This tool only works for pokemon naturally found in the wild.

This locator works for all current pokemon versions!

To search for any pokemon, simply type their name or if you know their number in the pokedex:
